Welcome to Pulkit Restaurant

The best in the country

Pulkit Restaurant which started in 1947 after independence is still on going and providing a great taste to indians and their apetite. This beloved establishment began as a modest eatery with a vision to serve authentic Indian cuisine to a growing nation. Over the years, it has evolved into a renowned institution, celebrated for its commitment to quality and tradition.

Pulkit Restaurant is not just a place to dine; it is a destination where food and culture come together to create lasting memories. We invite you to experience the exceptional flavors and warm hospitality that have made us a cherished part of the Indian dining landscape for over 75 years.
